Views

There are 3 views that can be used to see your calendar:

The monthly view is simply the traditional monthly calendar. In this view, as many tasks as can fit are shown. To see all tasks, click on any day and you will betaken to the task view for that day.

The weekly view is a calendar zoomed in to just one week. It will show you all tasks for each day of the current week. It can be customized in settings to display the weekdays in boxes or dropdowns.

Task view a daily view, but with a twist. The current day will be shown on the left side. On the right side, your next 10 upcoming tasks (after today) are shown, so that you can start preparing for what you have to do next. Task view can also be customized to show differnt things in the right column, according to your preferences.
