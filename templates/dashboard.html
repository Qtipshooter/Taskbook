Views

There are 3 views that can be used to see your calendar. The first is the monthly view, which is simply the traditional monthly calendar. In this view, as many tasks as can fit are shown. To see all tasks, click on any day and you will be taken to the task view for that day. The second view is weekly view, which is a calendar zoomed in to just one week. It will show you all tasks for each day of the current week. The thris view is task view. This is a daily view, but with a twist. The current day and all tasks scheduled for that day will be shown on the left side. On the right side, your next 10 upcoming tasks (after today) are shown, so that you can start preparing for what you have to do next. Task view can also be customized to show differnt things in the right column, according to your preferences.
